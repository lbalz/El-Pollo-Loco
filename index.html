Gameplay Info

Use the arrow keys to move left and right, and the space bar to jump.

Collect coins and bottles while avoiding enemies!

Desktop Info

Use the keys A & D to move left and right, and the W or space bar to jump.

Collect coins and throw bottles with F while avoiding enemies!

Mobile Info

Use the left and right buttons to move, and the jump button to jump.

Collect coins and throw bottles with the hand button while avoiding enemies!
